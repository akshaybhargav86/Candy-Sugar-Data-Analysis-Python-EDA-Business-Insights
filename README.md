# Candy-Sugar-Data-Analysis-Python-EDA-Business-Insights
End-to-end data analysis project on candy products to explore sugar consumption patterns, category trends, and business insights using Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)


# 🍬 Candy Sugar Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing candy product data to understand sugar consumption patterns, category performance, and business insights. The goal is to perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and derive meaningful conclusions that can help businesses make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze sugar content across different candy categories
* Identify high and low sugar products
* Understand the relationship between sugar, price, and sales
* Discover trends and patterns in consumer preferences

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains information about various candy products, including:

* Candy Name
* Category (Chocolate, Gummies, Hard Candy, etc.)
* Sugar Content (grams)
* Price
* Sales
* Rating

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn

---

## 🔍 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

The following analysis was performed:

* Data cleaning and preprocessing
* Handling missing values
* Removing duplicates
* Statistical summary of dataset
* Category-wise analysis

---

## 📊 Key Analysis & Visualizations

* Distribution of sugar content
* Category-wise average sugar levels
* Top high-sugar candies
* Price vs Sugar relationship
* Sales vs Sugar correlation

---

## 💡 Business Insights

* Certain categories like chocolates and gummies tend to have higher sugar content
* High sugar products may show higher sales trends
* There is an opportunity for low-sugar or health-focused candy products
* Pricing strategies may be influenced by sugar content and product type

---

## 📈 Conclusion

This analysis highlights important trends in the candy market, especially around sugar consumption and product performance. Businesses can leverage these insights to optimize product offerings and target health-conscious consumers.
